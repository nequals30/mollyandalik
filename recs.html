Here's some

Recommendations



While you are in Saint Louis, you might want to experience some of Molly and Alik's favorite attractions, including the many excellent free museums! Maybe you want to try some of their favorite places to eat and drink? If so, this (non-exhaustive) list is for you.

Forget the gooey butter cake and the so-called “square beyond compare,” and experience the real culinary delights of Saint Louis!

Coffee Shops


Blueprint Coffee
6225 Delmar Blvd, Saint Louis, MO 63130
Some of the best espresso drinks in Saint Louis.

Coffeestamp
2511 S Jefferson Ave, Saint Louis, MO 63104
Coffee and empanadas!

Comet Coffee
5708 Oakland Ave, Saint Louis, MO 63110
Go here for great pastries and quiche with your coffee.

Fiddlehead Fern Cafe
4066 Russell Blvd, Saint Louis, MO 63110
A very cute cafe in walking distance from Tower Grove Park and the Missouri Botanical Garden.

Kaldi's Coffee
700 De Mun Ave, Saint Louis, MO 63105
Molly and Alik’s favorite local chain coffee shop. You can find the couple there pretty much every Friday morning. The original Demun location has an entirely vegetarian menu and is right across from Concordia Park, a nice place to stroll.

The Mud House
2101 Cherokee St, Saint Louis, MO 63118
An excellent place for brunch or coffee on Cherokee street. Try the slinger, a Saint Louis classic!

Restaurants and Bars


Corner 17
6623 Delmar Blvd, Saint Louis, MO 63130
Where Molly and Alik frequently go for Chinese food. Try the homemade noodles, dumplings, and bubble tea.

Louie
706 De Mun Ave, Clayton, MO 63105
Great place in Molly and Alik’s neighborhood for Italian pizza, wine, and cocktails.

Meskerem Ethiopian Restaurant
3210 S Grand Blvd, Saint Louis, MO 63118
Molly and Alik’s favorite Ethiopian food.

Pizza Head
3196 S Grand Blvd, Saint Louis, MO 63118
Awesome pizza by the slice or the pie. The vegan pepperoni are premium.

Pizzeria da Gloria
2024 Marconi Ave, Saint Louis, MO 63110
Some of the best Italian pizza and salad. Highly recommended for dining in the Hill.

Planter's House
1000 Mississippi Ave, Saint Louis, MO 63104
The best cocktails in the city, hands down! A special place for Molly, Alik, and friends.

Seoul Taco
6665 Delmar Blvd, Saint Louis, MO 63130
Long considered one of the top food trucks in Saint Louis, Seoul Taco now has a brick-and-mortar location on the loop that serves excellent Korean-inspired burritos, tacos, and bowls.

Tai Ke Shabu Shabu
9626 Olive Blvd, Olivette, MO 63132
Super delicious Taiwanese food.

Terror Tacos
3191 S Grand Blvd, Saint Louis, MO 63118
Metal music and horror themed vegan taco shop on South Grand.

The Royale Food & Spirits
3132 S Kingshighway Blvd, Saint Louis, MO 63139
An excellent, neighborhood bar with great cocktails and a back courtyard. Extra points if you can name every historical figure whose portrait hangs on the wall.

Union Loafers Café and Bread Bakery
1629 Tower Grove Ave, Saint Louis, MO 63110
The best bread and best lunch in the city. The cheesy bread and little gem salad are highly addictive.

Vicia
4260 Forest Park Avenue, Saint Louis, MO 63108
In case you are feeling something a bit upscale. This place serves vegetable-focused, local cuisine in the Cortex innovation Community.

Attractions


Climb So iLL at the Power Plant
1419 Carroll St, Saint Louis, MO 63104
An indoor climbing gym inside the historic power plant building of the Old City Hospital complex. Molly and Alik have climbed here for many years. If that sounds like something you would like to try, you can get a day pass and rent your gear too. Afterwards have a drink upstairs at the Bellwether!

Contemporary Art Museum St. Louis
3750 Washington Ave, Saint Louis, MO 63108
A great free museum for viewing contemporary art exhibitions.

Forest Park
One of the largest city parks in the United States and the location of the Jewel Box, where Molly and Alik's wedding celebration will take place.

Mildred Lane Kemper Art Museum
1 Brookings Dr, Saint Louis, MO 63130
A great free art museum on the campus of Washington University.

Missouri Botanical Garden
4344 Shaw Blvd, Saint Louis, MO 63110
A 79-acre complex of gardens first opened to the public in the Victorian era. The Missouri Botanical Garden is now also a site for scientific research on plant diversity.

Pulitzer Foundation for the Arts
3716 Washington Ave, Saint Louis, MO 63108
A great free art museum for special exhibitions. A must-see for fans of contemporary architecture!

Saint Louis Art Museum
1 Fine Arts Dr, Saint Louis, MO 63110
A free encyclopedic art museum on Art Hill in Forest Park.

Tower Grove Park
A lovely Victorian park next to the Missouri Botanical Garden. The park holds a popular farmer's market on Saturday mornings in the summer.
