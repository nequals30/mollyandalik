Are children invited to the wedding?

We love your little ones, but our wedding is an adults-only event because of limited capacity at the venue. Please do bring your children to the rehearsal dinner for out-of-town guests and the breakfast the following morning!

For childcare, contact TLC Family Care by June 1st. They offer one-time appointments for a $40 staffing fee and $17 per hour.


Do you have a gift registry?

All we wish for is your presence; no gifts are necessary. We are so thrilled that you are able to make it to our celebration!

If you would still like to give a gift, please consider contributing to one of your favorite charitable organizations or to one of ours: St. Patrick's Center or Arch City Defenders.


What should I wear?

For the wedding, we suggest summer cocktail attire or whatever you feel best in! The wedding will take place in an air-conditioned greenhouse and there will be an outdoor cocktail hour, weather permitting.

The rehearsal dinner for out-of-town guests and the breakfast are casual.


How do I get to Urban Chestnut Midtown and where can I park?

The rehearsal dinner for out-of-town guests will take place in the Barrel Room at the Midtown location of Urban Chestnut brewery, so don't go to the Grove location, or you'll miss out!

Please park at one of two lots we have reserved across the street at Washington Tabernacle church. We will have signs up for the event.


How do I get to the Jewel Box and where can I park?

The Jewel Box is located at the intersection of McKinley and Wells Drives in Forest Park. A map can be found here. Free parking is available along the sides of McKinley and Wells Drives. We recommend arriving early since parking in Forest Park is in high demand on summer weekends.


How do I get to the Turkish Pavilion in Tower Grove Park and where can I park?

The Turkish Pavilion is located in Tower Grove park near the intersection of Center Cross Drive and Southwest Drive. A map of the park can be found here. Free parking is available throughout the park.


Is the Jewel Box wheelchair accessible?

The Jewel Box is a wheelchair-accessible venue. Guests with mobility needs can use the ramp at the venue entry.
